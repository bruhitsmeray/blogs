The Pain Month! - March

by Ray • April 3rd, 2023

What's new


March finally ended and I got the whole taste of the pain this month had to offer. This month, I managed to finish parts of my commission, write a bit and update Decay and some of the plugins.


I also managed to create some scenes for my Twitch streams that will come back consistently sometimes in the next months.



Decay


Starting on the 20th of March, I started working actively on Decay again and I gotta say, that in just a few days, I've made some pretty good progress. Managed to complete the Blockout for the Intro level, added some assets and logic for some stuff and fixed some other stuff.


I'm possitive that work on the Blockout for the House level will start in April. Maybe I'll even post a new YouTube video showcasing the progress!


HyperionUI


In consequence of Decay getting some love this month, HyperionUI got updated too. The plugin now has support for AMD FidelityFX™ Super Resolution 2.2. Your Upscaling settings get saved too (finally) when changing any of it's 3 settings.


Other Updates


I also managed to commit some updates to GitHub for: Aleph, HyperionCore, Konsole & Seekr.


Conclusion


March is done! Let April begin! See you next month!
