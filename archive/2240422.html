Hello everyone!


First things first, I want to let y'all know that this month I have managed to upgrade my CPU from a Ryzen 5 2600 to a Ryzen 9 5900x to help me with build times and I am extremely excited about this.


With that out of the way, let's talk game dev. This month I have made some decisions regarding the future development of Decay.

AI & UI

The remaining of this month, I will focus on refactoring some UI code. Furthermore, the CCTV UI has been reworked and simplified, allowing both KBM and Controller users to have an enjoyable experience while using them.


I've also continued work on Helper AI, making them reliable chasers. In the future, I will add the ability for them to also kill you when catching you.

Future Developments

After the UI code refactoring is complete, Decay will be separated into 2 branches. The "After Hours" branch and the Main branch. Decay: After Hours is a demo that will be released on GameJolt and later, on Steam for everyone to enjoy. Although the demo will feature a different story, the enemies and mechanics will be identical to the ones in the final release.


Even if the story will be smaller in scale, that doesn't mean that it will not give players some insight into the wider world of Decay. So when the demo will be released, keep an eye out, you might find something interesting.

Conclusion

I am not ready to give a final release date for Decay: After Hours yet, but I can say for certain that it will be released by Halloween. Stay tuned for more info on that.


See you safely on the other side.
